FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?+

It depends on scope. A landing page takes 1–2 weeks. A full website typically 4–8 weeks. A custom web or mobile application ranges from 2–6 months. We'll give you a precise timeline in the discovery phase.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?+

Absolutely. We work with clients across the US, UK, UAE, and beyond. Our team is remote-ready with tools and processes built for seamless international collaboration.

What do I need to get started?+

Just a rough idea of what you want to build! We handle the rest — from scoping and strategy to design and development. The more context you can share, the better, but we're great at working from rough briefs.

Will I own the code and design files?+

Yes, 100%. Upon project completion and final payment, all source code, design files, and assets are transferred to you. You own everything outright with no licensing restrictions.

Can you redesign or improve my existing website?+

Definitely. We do full redesigns, performance audits, and incremental improvements. We'll assess your current site and recommend the best path forward — sometimes that's a rebuild, sometimes it's targeted enhancements.
